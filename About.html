Why Choose TechSpecific: Charting My Journey

Welcome to TechSpecific, your dedicated partner for technology solutions. My journey, as founder Travis Hill, is one of resilience, adaptability, and an unwavering commitment to continuous improvement. I invite you to discover the milestones that have shaped TechSpecific.

2005: The Spark of a Sustainable Idea

In 2005, my interest in sustainability sparked the idea for EcoEffect, a sustainable housing concept. This venture, although not yet realized, planted the seed for my passion for technology and sustainability, forming the foundation of my future work.

2008: Expanding My Horizons

By 2008, I was exploring diverse fields, seeking out invaluable experiences that would shape the future of TechSpecific. The knowledge and insights gained during this period played a significant role in shaping my understanding of the ever-evolving technological landscape.

2013: Embracing Technology

In 2013, I fully embraced my passion for technology. Through various experiences and roles in industry-leading organizations, I delved deeper into the realm of technology and its relationship with sustainability. This marked the beginning of my specialized journey in the technology sector.

2018: Planting the Seeds of Entrepreneurship

In 2018, I conceptualized HarterHill Holdings, a testament to my growing entrepreneurial spirit and desire to lay a strong foundation for my family. In the same year, I deepened my expertise by engaging with Nextiva, a leading VoIP and SaaS provider. These experiences provided a rich source of inspiration for the future direction of TechSpecific.

2019: Establishing HarterHill and Expanding Technological Expertise

In 2019, I officially established HarterHill Holdings under an LLC. As I continued to grow professionally, I also expanded my expertise and understanding of cutting-edge technologies through my involvement with Vonage, a prominent company in the field of VoIP and Networking.

Early 2020: The Birth of TechSpecific and Navigating Challenges

Early 2020 was a period of resilience and innovation. In the face of the COVID-19 pandemic and the loss of a job opportunity at Apple, I founded TechSpecific. Though my aim was to focus on technology services, the demand for handyman services led me to split my focus. This marked a turning point as I navigated the balance between my technology aspirations and the realities of the gig economy.

2022: TechSpecific Reimagined

In April 2022, I redefined TechSpecific's purpose and direction. Harnessing my accumulated experiences and expertise, I focused on offering reliable and tailor-made technology solutions. This marked a significant turning point, solidifying TechSpecific's position as a trusted technology partner.

2023: A New Era of Excellence

Today, TechSpecific, officially LLC'd in April 2023, stands tall as a beacon of innovation and expertise in technology services. With our recent integration of AI capabilities, we have entered another transformative phase, positioning ourselves for even greater success. Our cutting-edge solutions are designed to demystify technology and empower our clients. We strive to make technology accessible and unleash its limitless potential. Meanwhile, my handyman services have found a home under the newly minted YoService.

Join Our Journey

As we reflect on our journey, we invite you to be a part of our future. Experience the power of TechSpecific as we deliver exceptional service, provide customized solutions, and tap into the limitless potential of technology. At TechSpecific, passion, expertise, and innovation converge to create meaningful and lasting value. From the inception of EcoEffect to the establishment of TechSpecific, my unwavering drive for innovation and sustainability propels us forward, solidifying our current position as a trusted technology partner. I invite you to join us in our quest to harness technology for a sustainable future.
